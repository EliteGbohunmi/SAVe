About Me

I’m Savvy Cross, a passionate motion designer dedicated to transforming ideas into dynamic visual stories. My journey into motion design began with a simple desire—to bring creativity to life through movement and animation. Over the years, I’ve honed my craft, specializing in creating motion graphic videos that not only captivate but also inspire and inform. My expertise lies in working with cutting-edge industries like fintech and Web3, where I help brands enhance their presence and connect with audiences through engaging content. Each project I take on is an opportunity to craft a unique narrative, turning complex concepts into visually stunning designs that resonate with viewers. Beyond motion design, I also manage and create video content for a wide range of projects, including memecoins, utility tokens, airdrops, and testnets. This role has allowed me to stay ahead of emerging trends, ensuring that my work remains fresh, relevant, and impactful in an ever-evolving digital landscape. You can experience the essence of my work in my 2024 showreel, a collection of projects that reflect my creative vision and dedication to quality: SHOWREEL 2024. Motion design is not just my profession—it’s my passion and a medium through which I bring ideas to life. I’m always striving to push the boundaries of what’s possible, delivering content that connects deeply with audiences and elevates brands to new heights.
